Why EaaS

EDI as a Service offers a comprehensive set of fully outsourced EDI services that helps companies to take full advantage of their EDI investment. With EaaS, companies are able to optimize the efficiency, reliability, and reach of their electronic supply chain while reducing costs, infrastructure and overhead. Future they also have the ability to let existing or new partners register and integrate with their own EDI smoothly and rapidly.

  • EaaS Portal - user interface of the EaaS platform
  • EaaS Core API - supports EaaS Portal for management of resources and organization units, manages relationship between partners in the EaaS platform
  • EaaS Runtime - current runtime implementation was based on Microsoft BizTalk Server 2010
  • Load balance - one organization was hosted on one BizTalk Server by default, however, in current design, multiple host servers and load balancing was also supported.
